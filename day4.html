Ingredients
  • Oyster Mushrooms
  • Shiitake
  • Frozen Dumplings
Directions
  1. Wash mushrooms
  2. Cook in oil until soft
  3. Add water and bring to boil
  4. Add frozen dumplings and cook for 8 minutes (or more depending on need)
  5. Add salt if desired
