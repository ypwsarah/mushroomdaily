Ingredients
  • Tomato Basil Pasta Sauce
  • Fusilli
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
Directions
  1. Bring water to boil and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add pasta and cook in medium heat to desired softness.
  3. Take pasta out from the water. Stir pasta with some olive oil.
  4. Heat tomato basil pasta sauce in frying pan.
  5. Add pasta and stir it with the sauce in the frying pan.
  6. Ready to serve!
