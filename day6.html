Ingredients
  • Shiitake
  • Tofu
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Egg
  • Carrot
  • Cooked Rice
  • Salt
Directions
  1. Wash ingredients
  2. Cut tofu into cubes and slice mushrooms, carrots and green bell pepper
  3. Cook shiitake in oil
  4. Add green bell pepper
  5. Scramble egg
  6. Add carrot
  7. Season with salt and desired flavorings
  8. Serve with rice
