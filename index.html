Simple Cooking

This website records the creator's journey of integrating mushrooms into her daily cooking practices. There are various very basic approaches that we can cook with mushrooms. The creator wishes to share simple recipes that anyone could follow, even on busy days, to easily make mushrooms a part of our everyday lives. By recording her own experiences and by presenting mushrooms in an engaging manner, the creator hopes to foster a connection with people who may be new to fungi as food and reduce any fears and anxieties about cooking with mushrooms.
