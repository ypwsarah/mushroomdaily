Ingredients
  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
Directions
  1. Wash the tomatoes and cut them into small pieces.
  2. Crack and beat desired amount of eggs in a bowl.
  3. Heat olive oil in frying pan.
  4. Add the eggs to the pan and scramble lightly. Take it out and set aside.
  5. Cook the tomatoes until the juice comes out.
  6. Add the eggs back to the pan along with some flavourings.
  7. Ready to serve with rice!
