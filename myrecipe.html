Ingredients
  • Oyster Mushrooms
  • Shiitake
  • Brown Beech
  • Tofu
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Salt
Directions
  1. Wash mushrooms
  2. Heat olive oil in frying pan and add the mushrooms
  3. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes until softened and sizzling
  4. Cut tofu into cubes
  5. Add water and bring to boil
  6. Add tofu and sliced cherry tomatoes
  7. Season with salt
