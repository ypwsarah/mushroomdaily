Ingredients
  • White Button Mushrooms
  • Yellow and Orange Bell Peppers
  • Egg
  • Cooked Rice
  • Seasoned Laver
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
Directions
  1. Slice button mushrooms and peppers
  2. Cook in oil until soft
  3. Crack and beat eggs in a bowl
  4. Add the eggs and scramble
  5. Add refrigerated overnight rice
  6. Finish with desired seasonings
